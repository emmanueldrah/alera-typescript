## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Sign Up**
   - Click "Sign Up" on the login page
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password (recommended: 8+ characters with mixed case)
   - Select your role (Patient, Doctor, Pharmacist, or Administrator)
   - Verify your email address (if enabled)

2. **Logging In**
   - Visit the ALERA login page
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"
   - You'll be directed to your personalized dashboard

3. **First Time Setup**
   - Complete your profile with personal/professional information
   - Set your preferences (notifications, privacy settings)
   - Review the Patient Consent & HIPAA compliance acknowledgment (if patient)

## Core Features

### 1. Appointment Management

#### For Patients:
1. **Schedule an Appointment**
   - Click "Appointments" in the sidebar
   - Click "Schedule New Appointment"
   - Select doctor/provider
   - Choose date and time from available slots
   - Add reason for visit (optional)
   - Click "Book Appointment"

2. **View Your Appointments**
   - See upcoming and past appointments
   - View appointment details (date, time, provider, location)
   - Access appointment reminders

3. **Cancel or Reschedule**
   - Find the appointment in your list
   - Click "Cancel" or "Reschedule"
   - Provide a reason for cancellation (required)
   - Select new date/time for rescheduling
   - Confirm the change

4. **Appointment Reminders**
   - Receive notifications 24 hours before appointment
   - Reminders sent via SMS or email (based on preference)
   - Click reminder to confirm attendance

#### For Doctors:
1. **View Patient Appointments**
   - Access doctor schedule in dashboard
   - See waiting room status
   - View patient information before appointment

2. **Confirm Appointments**
   - Click appointment from your schedule
   - Review patient details and medical history
   - Click "Confirm Appointment" to validate attendance

3. **Add Clinical Notes**
   - After appointment, click "Add Clinical Notes"
   - Record observations, diagnosis, and treatment plan
   - Attach documents if needed
   - Save to patient's medical record

### 2. Medical History & Health Records

#### Viewing Medical History
1. Click "Medical History" in the sidebar
2. Browse records organized by category:
   - Active Conditions
   - Medical History
   - Surgical History
   - Vaccinations
   - Family History
   - Social History

#### Adding Medical Records
1. Click "Add Medical Record"
2. Select record type
3. Fill in relevant information
4. Add dates and provider information
5. Save record

**Record Types Tracked:**
- Medical Conditions (with status: active/chronic/resolved)
- Surgical procedures (surgeon, hospital, date)
- Vaccinations (vaccine name, date, next due)
- Family medical history (genetic predispositions)
- Social history (smoking, alcohol, drug use)

---

### 3. Allergy Management

#### Recording Allergies
1. Navigate to "Allergies" in the dashboard
2. Click "Add Allergy"
3. Enter allergy details:
   - **Allergen Name**: What you're allergic to
   - **Type**: Medication, Food, Environmental, or Latex
   - **Severity**: Mild, Moderate, or Severe
   - **Reaction**: Describe your allergic reaction
   - **Status**: Active, Suspected, or Resolved

4. Add additional notes if needed
5. Click "Save Allergy"

#### Allergy Safety Features
- All your allergies appear prominently in your medical record
- Doctors see allergy alerts when viewing your profile
- System automatically warns before prescribing medications you're allergic to
- Pharmacists are notified of allergies when processing prescriptions

**⚠️ Important:** Always notify healthcare providers of new allergies immediately.

### 4. Prescription Management

#### Viewing Prescriptions
1. Click "Prescriptions" in the sidebar

**Refill Tracking:**

- The system automatically checks for dangerous drug combinations
- You'll see warnings if new prescriptions interact with current medications
- Discuss any warnings with your doctor before taking new medications

### 5. Prescription Refill Management

#### For Patients:
1. Click "Prescription Refills" in the dashboard
2. View all your prescriptions and refill status
3. **Request a Refill:**
   - Find the prescription
   - Click "Request Refill"
   - Add any notes for your pharmacist
   - Click "Submit"

4. **Track Refill Status:**
   - Pending: Waiting for pharmacist review
   - Approved: Ready to pick up
   - Dispensed: Already refilled

#### For Pharmacists:
1. Access "Prescription Refills" menu
2. View all pending refill requests
3. Click request to review:
   - Patient details
   - Current medications
   - Drug interactions
   - Allergy information

4. **Approve or Deny:**
   - Click "Approve" to process refill
   - Click "Deny" if refill unavailable or patient needs doctor visit
   - Add optional notes to patient

## Safety & Compliance

### HIPAA Compliance

ALERA is HIPAA compliant and protects your healthcare information:
- All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
- Access logs are maintained for all record access
- Patient consent is required before sharing information
- Regular security audits are performed

### Patient Consent & Privacy

When using ALERA, you agree to:
- Standard privacy practices for healthcare information
- Electronic communication with providers
- Secure storage of your health records
- Our privacy and data handling policies

**You can review and withdraw consent at any time** in Privacy Settings.

### Drug Interaction Safety

The system includes an automated drug interaction checker:
- **Severe Interactions**: Highlighted in red, requires doctor confirmation
- **Moderate Interactions**: Yellow warning, discuss with doctor
- **Minor Interactions**: Informational, monitor for symptoms

**Common Dangerous Interactions:**
- Warfarin + Aspirin (severe bleeding risk)
- SSRIs + MAOIs (serotonin syndrome)
- ACE Inhibitors + Potassium supplements (hyperkalemia)
- NSAIDs + ACE Inhibitors (kidney damage risk)

### Allergy Alerts

All recorded allergies are:
- Displayed prominently in your medical record
- Checked against all new prescriptions
- Visible to all your healthcare providers
- Used to prevent dangerous medications

**Critical:** Inform all providers immediately of new allergies.

## FAQ

### How do I reset my password?
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### How do I change my privacy settings?
1. Click "Settings" in the top right

3. Adjust who can see your information
4. Click "Save Settings"

### How long before an appointment do I get a reminder?
Reminders are sent 24 hours before your scheduled appointment time.

### Can I access my medical records from my phone?
Yes! ALERA is optimized for mobile devices. Visit the same website from your phone or download the mobile app.

### What should I do if I see an error on my medical record?
1. Click "Contact Support"
2. Report the error with details
3. Your provider and admin will be notified
4. We'll correct the record and notify you

### How can I share my records with another doctor?
1. Go to "Medical History"
2. Click "Share Records"
3. Select provider to share with
4. Click "Send"
The provider will receive a notification to accept access.

### What if I forget my patient ID?
Your patient ID is shown in your profile. Click your profile icon → "My Profile" to view it.

### How do I report a problem with the system?
1. Click "Help" in the sidebar
2. Click "Report an Issue"
3. Describe the problem and upload screenshots
4. Submit the report
5. Support team will contact you within 24 hours

### Can I use ALERA on multiple devices?
Yes! Your account works on all devices. Log in with your email and password on any device.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem:** "Invalid email or password" error

**Solutions:**
1. Check your email spelling (capitalization doesn't matter)
2. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check if CAPS LOCK is on
4. Clear browser cookies and try again
5. Try a different browser

### Not Receiving Notifications

**Problem:** Not getting appointment reminders or alerts

**Solutions:**
1. Check notification settings (Settings → Notifications)
2. Check spam/junk email folder
3. Verify your email address is correct (Profile → Email)
4. Ensure browser notifications are enabled
5. Check if your phone can receive SMS (if using SMS)

### Missing Medical Records

**Problem:** Can't find a medical record you added

**Solutions:**
1. Check search filters (filter by date or type)
2. Look in the correct dashboard section
3. Refresh the page
4. Check if you're logged in as the correct user
5. Contact support if record was recently added

### Slow Performance

**Problem:** ALERA is running slowly

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh your browser
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Close other browser tabs
4. Try a different browser
5. Check your internet connection speed

### Can't Schedule an Appointment

**Problem:** "No available slots" or unable to book

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you selected a date in the future
2. Check if doctor's schedule is full
3. Try another date or provider
4. Refresh and try again
5. Contact your provider directly

### Prescription Refill Not Processing

**Problem:** Refill request stuck or denied

**Solutions:**
1. Check refill count (may need new prescription)
2. Verify prescription hasn't expired
3. Check for drug interaction warnings
4. Contact your pharmacist
5. Request new prescription from doctor

### Allergy or Interaction Alert

**Problem:** See an alert or warning you're unsure about

**Solutions:**
1. Read the alert message carefully
2. Don't stop taking medication without consulting doctor
3. Contact your doctor or pharmacist immediately
4. They can review the interaction and advise you
5. Do not ignore severe interaction warnings

### Data Not Saving

**Problem:** Changes not appearing after saving

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Look for error messages
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Log out and log back in
5. Try a different browser

### Billing Issue

**Problem:** Incorrect charge or balance question

**Solutions:**
1. Review itemized bill carefully
2. Check insurance was properly applied
3. Contact billing department
4. Request itemized statement
5. Ask about payment plan options

### Contact Support

If you can't resolve an issue:

1. Click "Help" or "Support" in the app
2. Describe your problem in detail
3. Include error messages or screenshots
4. Provide your patient/provider ID
5. Support team will respond within 24 hours

**Emergency Issues:**
For urgent medical concerns, **call 911 or visit your nearest emergency room**. Do not rely on ALERA for emergencies.
